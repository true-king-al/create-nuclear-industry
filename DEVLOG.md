# Create Nuclear Industrys — Dev Tracking

## ✅ Completed

- **Uranium Fuel Rod** — placeable rod block that emits radiation particles, heats up when hit by them, glows and shifts color with temperature, melts to lava at 1000°C
- **Copper Heat Pipe** — item that links two rods together to share heat, renders as a copper-colored cable between them
- **Radiation particles** — travel through the world, bounce off solid blocks, get absorbed by dense materials, heat up rods they hit
